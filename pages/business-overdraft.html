Business Overdraft Guide

What is a Business Overdraft Loan?

A Business Overdraft Loan is a flexible credit facility that helps businesses access extra funds whenever needed. Instead of taking a fixed business loan amount, you can withdraw money beyond your current account balance up to a pre-approved overdraft limit. The best part is that you pay interest only on the amount you actually use and only for the number of days you use it.

A business overdraft facility is ideal for managing day-to-day cash flow, working capital needs, supplier payments, salary payouts, and unexpected business expenses. Unlike traditional business loans, there’s no need to borrow a lump sum all at once. You can withdraw funds, repay them, and use the limit again as required, giving your business complete financial flexibility and better cash flow control.
