Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

At KreditSeva, we are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the security of your personal and financial information. By using our website or submitting your information, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1 Who We Are

KreditSeva is a financial assistance and loan distribution platform that helps individuals and businesses connect with Banks, NBFCs, Fintech Lenders, and other regulated financial institutions for various credit products. Our services include:

  • Personal Loans & Business Loans
  • Debt Consolidation Loans
  • Balance Transfer Solutions
  • Credit Improvement Assistance
  • Financial Product Advisory Services

KreditSeva acts as a facilitator and does not guarantee loan approval, interest rates, credit limits, or loan disbursement.

2 Information We Collect

To evaluate your eligibility and provide relevant assistance, we may collect:

Personal Information

  • Full Name & Mobile Number
  • Email & Residential Address
  • Date of Birth
  • PAN & Aadhaar Number
  • Employment & Business Details
  • Income Information

Financial Information

  • Existing Loan Details
  • Credit Card Information
  • Outstanding Debt Obligations
  • Bank Account & Salary Info
  • Credit Score & Credit Report
  • Financial Statements

Technical Information

  • IP Address
  • Browser & Device Info
  • Location Data
  • Cookies
  • Website Usage Data

3 How We Use Your Information

The information collected may be used for:

  • Loan eligibility assessment and lender matching
  • Debt consolidation and balance transfer evaluation
  • KYC verification and fraud prevention
  • Customer support and regulatory compliance
  • Service improvement and marketing communication

4 Credit Score & Bureau Consent

By submitting any inquiry, application, or callback request through KreditSeva, you expressly authorize KreditSeva and its lending partners to access and retrieve your Credit Information Report, Credit Score, repayment history, and conduct risk assessment checks.

Authorized Credit Bureaus

  • CIBIL (TransUnion CIBIL)
  • Experian India
  • Equifax India
  • CRIF High Mark
Credit inquiries may be recorded by the respective credit bureau and may appear in your credit history. This consent remains valid until withdrawn by you or until completion of the application process.

5 Sharing Information with Lending Partners

To facilitate loan processing and financial assistance, you authorize KreditSeva to share your information with:

  • Banks, NBFCs & Fintech Lending Platforms
  • Credit Bureaus & KYC Agencies
  • Verification & Technology Service Providers
  • Collection and Recovery Partners (where applicable)

Such sharing shall be limited to legitimate business purposes related to evaluating, processing, servicing, or managing your financial product application.

6 Data Storage & Security

We implement industry-standard administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards including:

  • Encrypted storage systems and secure cloud infrastructure
  • SSL-encrypted website communication
  • Multi-level authentication and restricted employee access
  • Periodic security reviews and monitoring against unauthorized access
  • Backup and disaster recovery systems

While we take commercially reasonable measures to protect your information, no online platform can guarantee absolute security.

7 Data Retention

We retain personal and financial information only for as long as necessary to process applications, meet regulatory requirements, prevent fraud, resolve disputes, and fulfill contractual obligations. Information may continue to be retained after completion, rejection, or closure of an application where required by law.

8 Marketing & Communication Consent

By submitting your information, you consent to receive communications from KreditSeva and its authorized partners through Phone Calls, SMS, WhatsApp, RCS Messages, Email, and Push Notifications. These may include loan offers, debt consolidation programs, application status updates, service notifications, and promotional campaigns.

You may opt out of promotional communications at any time by contacting us.

9 Cookies Policy

KreditSeva uses cookies and similar technologies to improve website performance, enhance user experience, understand visitor behavior, and measure marketing effectiveness. Users can disable cookies through browser settings; however, some website functionality may be affected.

10 Your Rights

Subject to applicable laws, you may request:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdrawal of consent
  • Restriction of processing
  • Deletion of data where legally permissible

Requests may be submitted through our support channels.

11 Compliance with Indian Laws

KreditSeva endeavors to comply with applicable Indian laws and regulatory requirements, including:

  • Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023
  • Information Technology Act, 2000
  • Credit Information Companies (Regulation) Act, 2005
  • RBI Digital Lending Guidelines
  • KYC & AML Regulations

12 Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party lender websites and partner platforms. KreditSeva is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such third-party websites. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of external websites before sharing information.
