Grievance Redressal

We're here to listen. If something didn't go the way you expected, tell us — and we'll make it right.

You matter to us — not just as a customer, but as a person.

At KreditSeva, we take every concern seriously. Whether it's a delay in response, a miscommunication, or anything that left you feeling unheard — we want to know about it. Our grievance team is made up of real people who genuinely care about resolving your issue fairly and quickly.

What Can You Raise a Grievance About?

You can reach out to us for any of the following:

1

Service-related concerns

Delayed response, poor communication, or unsatisfactory support from our team.

2

Application or processing issues

Your inquiry was submitted but you didn't hear back, or there was confusion during the process.

3

Misinformation or miscommunication

You feel you were given incorrect or misleading information by any team member.

4

Privacy or data concerns

You have concerns about how your personal information was handled or shared.

5

Any other concern

If something felt off and you can't put it in a category — write to us anyway. We'll figure it out together.

How to Reach Us

You can contact our grievance team through any of the channels below. Please share your name, contact number, and a brief description of the issue so we can assist you faster.

How We Handle Your Complaint

Once we receive your grievance, here's what happens next:

Day 1

Acknowledgement

We confirm that we've received your complaint and share a reference number with you.

Within 3 Working Days

Initial Review

Our team reviews the details, speaks with the relevant person if needed, and begins working on a resolution.

Within 7 Working Days

Resolution

We aim to resolve most complaints within 7 working days. Complex cases may take a little longer — we'll keep you updated.

Closure

Feedback

We'll share the outcome with you and ask if you're satisfied. Your feedback helps us get better.

Still Not Satisfied?

If you feel your concern hasn't been resolved to your satisfaction after reaching out to us, you have the right to escalate the matter to the relevant regulatory authority or the lender/financial institution directly involved in your case.

We will always cooperate fully with any escalation process and provide all relevant information to support a fair resolution.

Our Promise to You

No complaint goes ignored at KreditSeva. Every concern is treated with respect, reviewed carefully, and responded to honestly. We may not always have the answer you hoped for, but we'll always give you a fair one.
