Our Story

Born to make
loans simple

KreditSeva was founded with one belief — getting a loan in India should not be painful. We watched millions of people navigate multiple bank visits, opaque interest rates, and endless paperwork, only to be rejected or overcharged. We decided to change that.

Today we are one of India's fastest-growing fintech platforms, connecting salaried professionals, business owners, doctors, CAs, and first-time borrowers with 90+ banks and NBFCs. Our experts compare every available offer and guide you to the right loan at the right rate — in minutes, not days.

What sets us apart is simple: we are 100% free for borrowers, always. We are compensated by lenders, so our only job is to find you the best deal.
