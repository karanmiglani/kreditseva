About KreditSeva

KreditSeva is a direct selling associate (DSA) operating as a unit of Voice Technologies and Business Solutions. We laid our foundation with the dream of making credit access in India simple, transparent, and fair — for every borrower, regardless of background. We are the bridge between you and India's leading banks and NBFCs, helping you find the right loan at the right terms, without the maze. Our team of advisers works with over 45 banking and NBFC partners — including HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Axis Bank, SBI, Bajaj Finserv, and Tata Capital — covering personal loans, business loans, professional loans, secured loans, and credit cards, all from a single platform with a single point of contact. We are not just a marketplace. We are your loan partner — from first enquiry to final disbursement.

Our Vision

KreditSeva was built with the vision to create a financially stable and financially aware India. We believe a loan should be a planned decision — not a burden, not a gamble, not a last resort forced on you by confusing paperwork and opaque lender policies.

We are reshaping how people access credit: by matching every borrower to the right lender before they apply, by staying with them through every step, and by measuring our success not in applications submitted but in lives genuinely improved.

The right loan at the right time can fund a business, secure a home, clear a burden, unlock a goal. Our job is to narrow the distance between that outcome and the person who deserves it.
