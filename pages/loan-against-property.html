Eligibility & Documents for Secured Loan

Eligibility Criteria for Secured Loan

Indian citizen with valid identity proof
Age between 21 to 58 years (as per lender policy)
Salaried, self-employed individuals, business owners, or professionals eligible to apply
Ownership of residential, commercial, or industrial property accepted by the lender
Stable income and repayment capacity
Healthy credit score (625+) preferred for better approval chances
Property should meet lender valuation and legal verification requirements

Documents Required for Secured Loan

PAN Card, Aadhaar Card, Passport / Voter ID
Address proof documents
Income proof (salary slips / business financial documents)
Last 6–12 months bank statements
ITR and income-related financial documents
Property ownership documents / title deed
Property tax receipts and utility bills (if required)
Recent passport-size photograph
