What is a Personal Overdraft?

A Personal Overdraft is a pre-approved revolving credit limit linked to your account. Unlike a regular loan, you don't borrow a lump sum — you draw funds as needed and repay them at your convenience.

The best part? You pay interest only on the amount you actually use, not the entire sanctioned limit. KreditSeva helps you get the best overdraft limit from 10+ top banks and NBFCs — completely free.
