# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Broad mix of Indian borrowers: salaried professionals, self-employed individuals, and business owners (SMEs). They arrive on mobile or desktop, often mid-comparison, under some financial pressure. They need to decide quickly whether to trust this platform with their personal and financial details.

## Product Purpose

KreditSeva is an Indian loan marketplace (a unit of Voice Technologies and Business Solutions) that connects borrowers with 45+ banks and NBFCs for personal, business, home, car, and professional loans. Success is a qualified lead submitting an application — conversion is the north star, trust is the prerequisite.

## Brand Personality

Assured, Direct, Swift — a professional adviser who knows the system, cuts to what matters, and never wastes your time.

Emotional goal: visitors should feel both **safe** (legitimate, RBI-aware, real partner lenders) and **capable of moving fast** (simple steps, no jargon maze, quick approval signal).

## Anti-references

- **BankBazaar / PolicyBazaar** — cluttered comparison-portal aesthetics, overwhelming dense tables, too many simultaneous choices. KreditSeva presents a guided path, not a supermarket.

## Design Principles

1. **One clear next action per screen.** Never compete with yourself — each section has a single CTA it earns.
2. **Authority through restraint.** The deep navy does trust work passively; content doesn't need to shout credentials on every line.
3. **Speed as a visible quality.** Motion, form progression, and copy all signal "this will be fast." Friction is a brand failure.
4. **Show the human behind the tech.** Loan applications are stressful moments; warmth (real testimonials, real partner logos, human copy) counterbalances the corporate blue.
5. **Earned density.** Information density is fine when the user asked for it (eligibility tables, EMI calculator). On landing surfaces, breathe.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. Primary users are on mid-range Android phones on 4G; performance and readability matter more than visual flair. Ensure sufficient contrast on all text including muted/helper copy. Support reduced motion.
